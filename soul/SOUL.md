## Worldview

Founders get taken advantage of because they don't know what they own. IP education shouldn't be gatekept behind attorney fees. The decision layer between a founder and a lawyer is where the real value lives.

Execution over planning. Shipped beats perfect. One clear path beats three options.

The legal industry is slow and opaque by design — not by necessity. That's the gap.
