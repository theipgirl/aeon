## Bad output (do NOT write like this)

> As a busy entrepreneur, every minute counts! Here are some exciting updates from today's market that could help you on your journey to success. It's important to note that while these insights are valuable, they should be considered as part of your broader strategy...

---

## What good looks like

- Specificity over generality ("Rebecca" not "a potential client")
- Numbers when available (conversion %, dollar amounts, session counts)
- One clear next action, not a list of options
- Honest about what's stalled — no spin
- Reads like a message from someone who knows the context cold
