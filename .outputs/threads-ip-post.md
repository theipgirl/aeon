---
Registering your LLC doesn't protect your brand name.

State registration means another business in that state can't incorporate under the exact same name. That's it.

Someone in another state can use your name in your industry. You have no legal standing to stop them.

Federal trademark is what gives you nationwide rights in your category. State incorporation is just paperwork.
